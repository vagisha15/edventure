What is Edventure?

Edventure is an AI teaching OS that turns goals into usable courses. Start with outcomes and constraints, then generate a full course shell that includes syllabus, weekly plan, materials, assignments, and grading. Iterate, compare versions, and export to your LMS in a few clicks.

Why are we building this?

Faculty want less busywork and more teaching time. Building a quality course takes weeks. Gathering materials, writing assignments, mapping outcomes, and formatting for each LMS creates constant friction. Edventure reduces the setup load and keeps instructors in control with clear version history and export paths.

What we provide

Course and syllabus generation
  • One-click course outline from goals, level, and term length
  • Week-by-week plan with outcomes, readings, labs, and pacing
  • Prerequisite mapping and dependency checks to prevent illogical sequencing
  • Multiple versions with compare and rollback
  • Import from existing syllabus, PDFs, or LMS course shells
Learning materials and assignments
  • Lecture outlines, reading lists, lab briefs, starter code, datasets
  • Assignment generator from topics or outcomes
  • Rubrics and grading guides auto-generated from prompts
  • Blueprint mapping from tasks to outcomes and topics
  • Import existing banks, de-duplicate, tag, and version
Quiz and paper builder
  • Quiz builder with MCQ, short answer, numeric, coding, and graphs
  • Difficulty tagging, Bloom's level tagging, randomization, question pools
  • Drag-and-drop question paper builder
  • Sectioning, marks allocation, negative marking
  • Export to PDF, DOCX, and LMS quiz formats
Grading and feedback
  • Auto-grading for MCQ, numeric, and code with test cases
  • AI-assisted rubric scoring for short answers
  • Plagiarism and similarity checks
  • Regrade request workflow and comment suggestions
  • Gradebook sync via LTI Assignment and Grade Services
Integrations and exports
  • LTI 1.3 adapters for Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard, Brightspace, and Schoology
  • One-click export to LMS quiz banks and course shells
  • Download as PDF and DOCX
  • APIs for roster, grade sync, and analytics
  • Security-first approach with clear audit trails
For departments
